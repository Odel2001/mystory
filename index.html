My 2024 Journey

A year filled with dreams, challenges, and unforgettable moments.

January 2024
The year began with a fresh sense of purpose and big life goals. I created a list of personal achievements I wanted to reach—whether it was improving my fitness, advancing my career, or simply finding more happiness in the everyday. I also started journaling regularly, which helped me reflect on my progress and kept me focused. One of my biggest goals was to grow mentally and emotionally, overcoming self-doubt and becoming the best version of myself.
March 2024
Basketball became a bigger part of my life than ever before. I joined a local league and spent hours on the court refining my skills. The thrill of competition pushed me to new heights, and every game taught me something valuable—not just about basketball but also about resilience, teamwork, and leadership. I even made a few new friends who shared my love for the sport.
April 2024
And then, everything changed—I met Jelly. It was during one of my weekend adventures when our paths crossed by chance. Jelly had this incredible energy and a smile that could light up a room. We spent hours talking about our shared love for travel and basketball. Every moment with her felt natural, like we had known each other forever. By the end of that month, I knew she was someone truly special.
July 2024
My love for adventure took me to breathtaking places. I traveled to remote destinations, camped under the stars, and climbed mountains that tested my limits. Each adventure was a chance to disconnect from the noise of daily life and reconnect with nature. The best part was bringing Jelly along on some of these trips—sharing those moments made them even more memorable.
October 2024
By the time autumn arrived, life felt like it had come full circle. My goals were becoming realities, and the people around me made the journey worth every step. Jelly became a constant presence in my life, someone who encouraged and inspired me every day. Together, we planned new adventures, supported each other through challenges, and built something truly beautiful—a connection I hadn’t imagined at the start of the year.
